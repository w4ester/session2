ğŸ’¡ Why tokens matter

LLMs process text as tokens (subwords/characters). Counting tokens helps you:

  • Estimate costs: Most AI APIs charge per token (~$0.03 per 1,000 tokens)
  • Manage limits: Models have context windows (8k-128k tokens)
  • Optimize speed: Fewer tokens = faster responses

2) Prompt Engineering Basics

Four principles: be specific, provide context, use examples, and break complex tasks into steps.

Key Principles

  1. Be specific and clear â€” specify format, tone, length, and audience.
  2. Provide context â€” share relevant background and constraints.
  3. Use examples â€” show a few samples of good outputs.
  4. Break tasks into steps â€” guide the model's reasoning.
